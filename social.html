What is Social Health?

Social Health encompasses a wide range of definition but can be broadly defined as how well one can have significant relationships and interact with others. Social Health Las measures how well one can adapt to different situations. This may include: Being at a party and not knowing many people, Office / School spaces or Lunches / Dinners. Social Health is important because as humans we are some if not the most social creatures by nature. On a day-to-day basis, social interaction makes up a vital part of human life.

Reflection

Spend time meditating on your relationships such as with the relationship with friends, families, co-workers and classmates. Think about how you feel about spending time with these people. Social Health however, is not always about people, it can also be the environments. So also take some time to think about how different environments make you feel? Are there any environments where you feel uneasy, and maybe a little bit apprehensive about? If and when you are able to identify these environment , its also important to understand why it is that you don’t feel conformable, or you don’t feel like yourself make sure. to try and identify why it is you feel that way?

Tips and Tricks!

Firstly, always try and remain authentic and true to yourself, no matter who you find yourself around. This can reduce the levels of anxiety and emotional restraint you may feel. Secondly, attempts should be made to improve your ability to communicate your emotions and feelings without restraint— This can be very hard for many people, both men and women. However the majority of human interaction is based on communication, and severed communication causes severed social health. Another tip would be to find a balance between your social and personal time! Your balance is not going to look like everyone else’s! Do not be afraid to cancel plans if you have to! Lastly, learn to lean on your friends and family for support. Make sure you have a good support system around you to support you during the tough times in life!
