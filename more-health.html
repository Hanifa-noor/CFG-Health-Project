Other Health Tips

Addiction

Many individuals fall victim to crippling dependencies or even addictions because they do not have the correct coping strategies or are too susceptible to peer pressure.
Try to abstain from an excessive intake of anything but especially drugs and alcohol.

Environment

One's environment has a larger effect than most people assume.
It is true that with the right mindset almost any situation can be bearable however that should not prevent you from always being open to change for the better, even if it involves cutting off people that aren't good for you or even moving elsewhere.

Passion Project

If you feel like your life lacks substance even if you're leading a supposedly "perfectly healthy" lifestyle consider finding a passion project.
Be it a new hobby, or a charitable cause you'd like to pursue, passion projects can give us the goals and motivation often rare in mundane routines.
